ABOUT THE TURBAN

33 YEARS OF EXCELLENCE..!!
The Turban Indian Restaurant will ensure you a perfect dining experience with its comfortable and casual atmosphere. This is THE perfect place for a Romantic night out or to spend some quality time with the family and friends, The scrumptious Meals created by our 5 star experienced chefs will be served to your table by our vibrant staff who will look after your dining needs throughout the night.

Our sister restaurant, AGNI FINE CUISINE, the ONLY purpose built majestic restaurant in Perth is situated in Innaloo. You could also head up north for a more majestic dining experience.
